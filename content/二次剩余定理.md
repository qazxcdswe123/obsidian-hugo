## What
- $(p-a)^{2} \equiv a^{2} \pmod{p}$
- $x^{2} = a \pmod{p}$ has either **no solutions** or **exactly 2 incongruent** solutions modulo p; when p and a coprime

## Properties
- $QR \times QR = QR$
- $QNR \times QR = QNR$
- $QNR \times QNR = QR$
